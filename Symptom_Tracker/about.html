About Symptom Tracker

Symptom Tracker is a web application built to help users easily log and monitor their health symptoms. Whether you're managing a chronic illness or just keeping an eye on daily well-being, this tool helps you keep accurate records over time.

Features include symptom logging, severity tracking, data visualization, and export capabilities. Your personal data is stored locally for privacy and can be shared with healthcare providers when needed.
